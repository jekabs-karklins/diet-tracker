Pre-Workout Meal:

Whole Grain Cereal with Milk and Strawberries:

  • High-fiber cereal (1 cup)
  • Low-fat milk or plant-based milk (1 cup)
  • Sliced strawberries (½ cup)

Post-Workout Meal:

Berry Protein Smoothie:

  • Mixed berries (1 cup)
  • Low-fat yogurt (½ cup)
  • Water or milk (½ cup)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)

Instructions: Blend until smooth.

Lunch:

Quinoa Salad with Chickpeas and Veggies:

  • Quinoa (1 cup cooked)
  • Chickpeas (½ cup)
  • Diced cucumber and tomatoes (1 cup)
  • Olive oil and lemon dressing (1 tbsp olive oil + juice of ½ lemon)

Instructions: Mix all ingredients.

Dinner:

Baked Chicken with Roasted Sweet Potatoes and Green Beans:

  • Chicken breast (5 oz)
  • Sweet potato (1 medium)
  • Green beans (1 cup)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp) for roasting

Instructions: Bake chicken and roast veggies in oven.
