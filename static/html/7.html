Pre-Workout Meal:

Smoothie Bowl with Granola:

  • Spinach (1 cup)
  • Banana (1 medium)
  • Almond milk (1 cup)
  • Granola (¼ cup)

Instructions: Blend spinach, banana, and milk; top with granola.

Post-Workout Meal:

Protein Shake with Berries:

  • Protein powder (1 scoop)
  • Water or milk (1 cup)
  • Frozen berries (½ cup)

Instructions: Blend until smooth.

Lunch:

Turkey and Vegetable Stir-Fry with Brown Rice:

  • Lean ground turkey (4 oz)
  • Mixed vegetables (1½ cups)
  • Brown rice (½ cup cooked)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp) for stir-frying

Instructions: Stir-fry turkey and veggies; serve over rice.

Dinner:

Grilled Salmon with Steamed Asparagus and Quinoa:

  • Salmon fillet (5 oz)
  • Asparagus (1 cup)
  • Quinoa (½ cup cooked)

Instructions: Grill salmon; steam asparagus.
