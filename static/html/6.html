Pre-Workout Meal:

Overnight Oats with Berries:

  • Rolled oats (½ cup)
  • Chia seeds (1 tbsp)
  • Low-fat milk or plant-based milk (1 cup)
  • Fresh berries (½ cup)

Instructions: Mix oats, chia seeds, and milk in a jar; refrigerate overnight. Add berries in the morning.

Post-Workout Meal:

Apple Cinnamon Smoothie:

  • Apple (1 medium, cored)
  • Cinnamon (½ tsp)
  • Oats (¼ cup)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)

Instructions: Blend until smooth.

Lunch:

Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast with Quinoa:

  • Chicken breast (5 oz)
  • Spinach (1 cup)
  • Feta cheese (1 oz)
  • Quinoa (½ cup cooked)

Instructions: Stuff chicken with spinach and feta; bake until cooked.

Dinner:

Baked Cod with Roasted Brussels Sprouts and Wild Rice:

  • Cod fillet (5 oz)
  • Brussels sprouts (1 cup)
  • Wild rice (½ cup cooked)

Instructions: Season cod with lemon and herbs; bake with Brussels sprouts.
