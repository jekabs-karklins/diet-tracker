Pre-Workout Meal:

Greek Yogurt with Berries:

  • Non-fat Greek yogurt (1 cup)
  • Fresh berries (½ cup)
  • Optional: Drizzle of honey (1 tsp)

Post-Workout Meal:

Mango Spinach Smoothie:

  • Mango chunks (1 cup)
  • Spinach (1 cup)
  • Coconut water (1 cup)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)

Instructions: Blend until smooth.

Lunch:

Lentil Soup with Whole Grain Bread:

  • Lentils (1 cup cooked)
  • Vegetables (carrots, celery, onions; 1 cup)
  • Vegetable broth
  • Whole grain bread (1 slice)

Instructions: Simmer lentils and veggies in broth until tender.

Dinner:

Grilled Shrimp with Asparagus and Quinoa:

  • Shrimp (5 oz)
  • Asparagus spears (1 cup)
  • Quinoa (½ cup cooked)

Instructions: Grill shrimp and asparagus; season with herbs.
