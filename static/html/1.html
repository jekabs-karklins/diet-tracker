Meal Plan

Pre-Workout Meal (30 minutes before exercise):

Whole Grain Oatmeal (1 cup cooked):

  • Prepare quick oats with water or milk.
  • Optional: Add sliced banana (½ medium) and a sprinkle of cinnamon for flavor.

Post-Workout Meal (Within 1 hour after exercise):

Green Protein Smoothie:

  • Spinach (1 cup)
  • Frozen mixed berries (1 cup)
  • Unsweetened almond milk (1 cup)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth.

Lunch:

Grilled Chicken Salad:

  • Grilled chicken breast (4 oz)
  • Mixed greens (2 cups)
  • Cherry tomatoes (½ cup)
  • Cucumber slices (½ cup)
  • Olive oil and lemon juice dressing (1 tbsp olive oil + juice of ½ lemon)

Dinner:

Baked Salmon with Steamed Broccoli and Quinoa:

  • Salmon fillet (5 oz)
  • Broccoli florets (1 cup)
  • Quinoa (½ cup cooked)

Instructions: Season salmon with herbs; bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes. Steam broccoli.
