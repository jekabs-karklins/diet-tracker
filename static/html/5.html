Pre-Workout Meal:

Whole Grain Toast with Peanut Butter and Banana:

  • Whole grain bread (1 slice)
  • Natural peanut butter (1 tbsp)
  • Sliced banana (½ medium)

Post-Workout Meal:

Pineapple Kale Smoothie:

  • Pineapple chunks (1 cup)
  • Kale leaves (1 cup)
  • Water or coconut water (1 cup)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)

Instructions: Blend until smooth.

Lunch:

Tuna Salad with Mixed Greens:

  • Tuna (canned in water, 4 oz)
  • Mixed greens (2 cups)
  • Cherry tomatoes (½ cup)
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing (1 tbsp)

Instructions: Combine ingredients.

Dinner:

Grilled Lean Steak with Sautéed Spinach and Brown Rice:

  • Lean steak (5 oz)
  • Spinach (2 cups)
  • Brown rice (½ cup cooked)
  • Olive oil (1 tsp) for sautéing

Instructions: Grill steak; sauté spinach with garlic.
