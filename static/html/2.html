Pre-Workout Meal:

Whole Grain Toast with Almond Butter:

  • Whole grain bread (1 slice)
  • Almond butter (1 tbsp)

Post-Workout Meal:

Banana Oat Smoothie:

  • Banana (1 medium)
  • Rolled oats (¼ cup)
  • Low-fat milk or plant-based milk (1 cup)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)

Instructions: Blend until smooth.

Lunch:

Turkey and Avocado Wrap:

  • Whole wheat tortilla (1 medium)
  • Sliced turkey breast (4 oz)
  • Avocado slices (¼ medium avocado)
  • Lettuce and tomato

Instructions: Assemble ingredients in tortilla.

Dinner:

Stir-Fried Tofu with Mixed Vegetables and Brown Rice:

  • Firm tofu (4 oz)
  • Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli; 1½ cups)
  • Brown rice (½ cup cooked)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp) for stir-frying

Instructions: Stir-fry tofu and vegetables; serve over rice.
